OUR PROJECT

Our project focuses on introducing a personalized fitting recommendation feature, addressing the lack of such tools on major e-commerce platforms in Vietnam like Shopee or Lazada. By leveraging this feature, we aim to enhance the shopping experience, helping customers find better-fitting products effortlessly, and reduce size-related returns for sellers.
